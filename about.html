About Us

BuyTheReel.com is your best place to shop for historic films and videos. The films and videos featured here are from the collection of an Emmy Award-winning team of filmmakers who, over the last three decades, have produced thousands of hours of premium documentary programming for networks like National Geographic, Discovery, the History Channel, and PBS. From these past productions, we have stored and cataloged a vast library of over 10,000 hours of original footage and archival material that is in the public domain or owned outright. New content is added every week, so check back often!
