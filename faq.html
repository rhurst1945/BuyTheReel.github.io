Frequently Asked Questions

There are many stock footage websites. What makes yours different?
At BuyTheReel.com, we believe in selling you the entire film! Other sites only give you a snippet of footage, i.e. 90 seconds worth. But our films are completely unedited, just as they came from the orginal film reel! And best of all, you get the entire film for one low price!
Where do you get your films?
All of our films are from the US government. They come from the National Archives, the Library of Congress, and the Department of Defense.
Are your films copyrighted?
No. Because all of our films are works of the United States Federal Government, they are considered to be public domain. That means that anybody can use them, however they see fit.
After purchasing a film, how do I get it?
All of our films are converted to .mp4 video format and delivered to you by FTP. After submitting payment, you'll receive a link to download the file. They range in size from 500 MB to 3 GB.
